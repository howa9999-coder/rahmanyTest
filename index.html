1.Steering & Sailing Rules (COLREGs)

Master right-of-way like a seasoned captain. Rahmany’s rulebook breaks down COLREGs into interactive scenarios—animate crossing situations, sound signal quizzes, and fog navigation drills. Our ‘Rule Engine’ auto-flags violations in user-plotted routes.

Animated GIFs/Videos of Rule 5–19 applications

2.NavFormulas: Loxo & Ortho Essentials

Master the two core paths of marine navigation—straight-line loxodromes (constant bearing) and curve-minimizing orthodromes (great circles)—with Rahmany’s step-by-step solvers. Learn to calculate distances, intermediate points, and course angles using spherical trigonometry or simplified Mercator approximations, tailored for cadets and coastal navigators.

Basic Coordinate Math
Trig-free coastal navigation shortcuts
Solve for constant-bearing routes with Mercator projection formulas
Haversine formula for shortest-path distance
